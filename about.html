About Me

I'm an aspiring software developer currently honing my skills at Emerging Digital Academy. With a passion for problem-solving and a knack for logic, I'm dedicated to excelling in the dynamic realm of technology.

My journey into software development may seem unconventional, but it's rooted in years of hands-on leadership experience. Over the past two decades, I've served as an owner/operator of multiple successful restaurants. Through these ventures, I've developed invaluable skills in team management, strategic planning, and operational efficiency.

Now, I'm leveraging my diverse background to pursue a career in software development. I thrive in environments that challenge me to think critically and creatively, and I'm eager to apply my problem-solving prowess to tackle complex technical challenges.

Outside of coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, attending industry meetups, and continuously expanding my knowledge base. I'm committed to lifelong learning and staying ahead of the curve in an ever-evolving field.
